MAK PARAGLIDING

MAK Alpinism provides a safe and secure paragliding experience to the adventurous ones. The one who love the heights and skies, the one who loves to fly and experience like a bird is always welcome to our MAK Alpinism Paraglide. Paragliding is the recreational and competitive adventure sport of flying paragliders. Sky diving, hang gliding and speed flying are some of the types of paragliding. A paraglider, a helmet, a reserver and a harness is required for paragliding.
